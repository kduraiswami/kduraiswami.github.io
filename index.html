Kailash Duraiswami

I launched an investment advisory firm after graduating college in January 2011 and generated portfolio returns in the top 1% of all money managers during the 3-year time period ending December 2013. I took time off in Australia in 2014 and realized what I was building did not fit in with my long-term happiness. It is difficult, and frightening, to gain a new skill set and identify what I was looking for in my next step. I realized a few crucial points that I wanted to focus on what I do next, teamwork and speed.

The teams we work with are probably the greatest source of joy or frustration in our professional lives.
